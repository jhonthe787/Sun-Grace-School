About Us

We had our humble Starting in the year 1997 under

Mr. Jaysukh Bhai Kathiriya,

a man of high ideas is the Pivot of this institution. Thirteen years of diligent work and Sacrifice has made us the proud owners of this institution.

The school is a co-educational institution affiliated to Gujarat State Education Board. We have got two mediums Gujarati and English with more than 2800 students. We conduct classes from Nursery to 12th standard.

The dream of having our own school building was fulfilled in 2003. We have got a magnificent structure which is earth quake resistant.

The ground floor is adorned by a statue of goddess Saraswathi and consists of Principal’s office, Reception, Clerk’s office, Staff room and management room.

Our developmental centres like Computer room, Audio visual room and Laboratory beautify the underground.

First & Second floor is the wonderland of our little stars. The main stream education goes on in the third and fourth floors. Under ground is the library of books & toys.

Airy and well lit class rooms with CC Cameras with recording are our attraction. Absolutely clean and hygienic environment within entire campus is maintained. This is the unique institution with all facilities one can dream of.

Special Feature

Trained Teachers :
Well qualified, trained and experienced teaching staff to inspire the young minds. Regular training programmes to upgrade the knowledge of teachers by experts.

Computer Education :
We have one of the most modern computer lab. Computer practice is provided to each and every student from 1st standard.

Library :
A well developed library with good collection of books and magazines to enrich the knowledge

Extra Curricular Activities :
In the quest for developing a well rounded personality, the school encourage the students to participate in variety of activities and competitions like drawing, quiz, singing, story-writing, debate, essay writing, Rangoli, Garba, Sloka etc.

Celebration of all the Festivals :
To maintain unity in diversity we celebrate all the main festivals of our country such as Independence Day, Republic Day. Sports Day, Children’s Day, Teacher’s Day, Navratri, Dushera, Diwali, Holi, Janmashtmi, Christ

Transport Facility :
Door to door transport facility to all the students of the school.

Music & Dance classes :
Music and dance teachers are engaged to train the students. We are conducting various competitions of dance and music to encourage the students. Our students also participate in outside competition.
